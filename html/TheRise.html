The Rise of Flash

Beginning in 1996, Flash Player was a computer software you had to download and ran on most internet browswers. In the early 2000's, Flash Player was a very common format for web games, animations and graphical user interface (GUI) it was the main support for many pc games such as: Poptropica, Club Penguin, Dino Run, Papas Pizzeria and many more. According to Adobe, in 2013 more than 400 million desktops updated to new versions of Flash Player quickly after updates were released. Club penguin was played by more than 330 million people, there were several minigames and side quests. You could even have a pet; the puffle! These furry creatures could be caught playing minigames, bought at the pet store, fed, played with , and named!
