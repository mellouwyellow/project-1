The Fall of Flash

Flash player ran for more than 20 years. Supporting hundreds of different online games for kids to play and interact with their friends online. I remember downloading Flash player on new family computers we got as a kid just so I could play my favorite games. Ultimately, the end of flash player was due to security reasons. Many games, streaming browsers and more used Flash player, which made it a target for hackers, forcing adobe to release frequent updates to meet these problems. They had no choice but to switch to a new software which led to the "death"" of Flash Player on December 31st 2020.
